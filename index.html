4 Guesses Remaining




The Rules

The technowizard's have recruited you, young mage, to divine a number from the Great Beyond! Use your technowizardry to divine this number (from 1 through 20) with the roll of this divining stone. You are to guess the number exactly lest our realm be overtaken by chaos (or yknow, you won't win the game). You have 4 attempts before chaos wins!
